# Controlled Pilot Charter

## Scope

Controlled internal pilot for GRC Control Center using synthetic/non-confidential data only.
Pilot limited to approved internal users only. No real patient identifiers. No confidential OVR details. No production rollout.

## Objectives

1. Validate workflow usability.
2. Validate role access behavior.
3. Validate evidence capture and reporting.
4. Validate operator support model.
5. Prepare go/no-go evidence for future production-readiness review.

## Non-goals

- No production deployment.
- No real patient data.
- No confidential OVR data.
- No bypass of v66 strict proof.
