# Governance, Risk & Action Control Center - Product Blueprint

## Purpose

A company-wide control platform for Al Modawat to manage major projects, governance actions, risk mitigation, compliance obligations, audit findings, policies, committee decisions, evidence, approvals and delays.

This is not a normal task tracker. It is a control-based platform.

## Core Chain

Source → Risk / Compliance / Audit / Decision / Policy Gap → Action Plan → Milestone → Task → Evidence → Approval → Closure

## Main Modules

1. Executive Dashboard
2. Projects & Action Plans
3. Milestones & Tasks
4. Evidence Center
5. Approvals
6. Risk Register
7. Risk Controls
8. Risk Mitigation Actions
9. Compliance Calendar
10. Internal Audit Follow-up
11. Policy Register
12. Authority Matrix
13. Committee Meetings & Decisions
14. Admin Organization Structure
15. Users, Roles & Access Scopes

## Key Rules

- Every major action must have an owner.
- Every major action must have a due date.
- Delayed items require a delay reason.
- Controlled closure requires evidence.
- Evidence must be reviewed and accepted.
- Audit findings cannot be closed by the department alone.
- High-risk overdue actions must appear in the executive dashboard.
- Normal employees see assigned work only.
- Executives and GRC roles see company-wide control dashboards.

## Rollout Plan

### Phase 1
Action plans, milestones, tasks, evidence, approvals and dashboard.

### Phase 2
Risk register, controls and mitigation actions.

### Phase 3
Compliance calendar and audit findings.

### Phase 4
Policies, authority matrix, committees and CEO decisions.

### Phase 5
Imports, exports, backup/restore, advanced notifications and analytics.
