# Signoff Record Retention SOP

## Principle

Manual signoff is part of controlled-pilot evidence and must be retained with the release evidence chain.

## Retention locations

- Source JSON approval files under `release/v674/approvals/`.
- Generated validation output under `release/v674/`.
- Proof suite output under `release/v700/proof-suite-all.json`.
- v9.2 QA outputs under `release/v92/`.

## Do not store

- Real patient identifiers.
- Confidential OVR details.
- Passwords or secrets.
- Screenshots containing confidential operational data.
