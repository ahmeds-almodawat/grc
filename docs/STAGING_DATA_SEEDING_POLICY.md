# Staging Data Seeding Policy

## Allowed

- Synthetic users.
- Synthetic departments.
- Synthetic OVR records.
- Synthetic evidence records.
- Synthetic risks and controls.

## Forbidden

- Real patient identifiers.
- Real confidential OVR details.
- Real employee sensitive data unless explicitly approved for pilot.
- Production database exports.
- Unredacted operational incidents.

## Labeling

All seeded records should clearly indicate synthetic or non-confidential pilot use.
