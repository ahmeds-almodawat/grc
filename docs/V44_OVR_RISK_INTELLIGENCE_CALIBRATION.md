# v4.4 OVR Risk Intelligence Calibration

Purpose: make OVR risk indicators useful and not noisy.

Risk indicator model:

- Severity weight
- Recurrence within 30 days
- Closure delay
- Evidence status
- Executive alert rules

Default seeded rules:

- Major OVR immediate executive alert.
- Sentinel event RCA and executive review.
- Repeated medication OVRs.
- Repeated falls/injury OVRs.
- Repeated environment-of-care OVRs.
- Overdue OVR closure signal.

Recommended calibration after pilot:

- Adjust recurrence threshold by department size.
- Separate near-miss reporting culture from harm signals.
- Review top recurring categories monthly.
- Track closure delays as governance/control weakness.
