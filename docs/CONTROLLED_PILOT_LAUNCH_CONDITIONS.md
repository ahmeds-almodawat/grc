# Controlled Pilot Launch Conditions

Controlled pilot can proceed only when:

1. Management/Admin signoff is complete and real.
2. IT signoff is complete and real.
3. Quality signoff is complete and real.
4. IT confidentiality confirmation is complete and real.
5. Quality confidentiality confirmation is complete and real.
6. Maximum pilot users is 1 to 15.
7. Pilot scope explicitly says controlled internal pilot.
8. No real patient identifiers are used.
9. No confidential OVR details are used.
10. `proof:all` passes after signoff.

Production remains out of scope.
