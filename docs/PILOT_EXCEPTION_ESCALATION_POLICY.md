# Pilot Exception and Escalation Policy

## Objective

Define how pilot exceptions are recorded, triaged, escalated, and closed.

## Exception levels

- Low: observation only
- Medium: requires owner and due date
- High: requires management/IT/Quality review
- Critical: pause affected pilot activity

## Escalation timing

- Critical: same day
- High: within 1 business day
- Medium: within 3 business days
- Low: in weekly review
